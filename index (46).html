Our Craft

The Art of Transformation

01

Premium Hair Architecture & Cuts

Every cut is a structural decision — a precise dialogue between face geometry, hair texture, and personal identity. Martina's bespoke consultation process maps the unique architecture of each client before a single scissor movement is made. From razor-sharp geometric bobs to ethereal layered cascades, each result is engineered to grow beautifully.

Consultation Included from 1 800 Kč
02

Advanced Technical Coloring & Balayage

The atelier specializes in hand-painted balayage, seamless root melt, and platinum blonde transformations executed with European precision. Our color architects use only professional-grade pigments — Wella Professionals, Goldwell Elumen, and L'Oréal Majirel — to deliver luminous, dimensional tones that honor the health of every strand.

Each color service includes a bespoke strand test, toning ritual, and a personalized maintenance protocol crafted for your specific hair biology. We do not rush the process — we honor it.

Custom Formula · 3-Step Process from 3 500 Kč
03

Deep Hair Reconstruction & Care

For hair compromised by chemical processing, heat, or environmental stress, we offer medical-grade reconstruction treatments: Olaplex bonding therapy, Brazilian keratin smoothing, and our signature Martina Ritual — a five-layer hydration and protein infusion protocol exclusive to the atelier.

Exclusive Atelier Protocol from 2 200 Kč

Meet the Specialists

M

Founder & Master Colorist

Martina Baštová

With over 15 years of practice across Prague, Paris, and London, Martina founded the atelier with a singular philosophy: every client deserves hair that is architecturally considered, technically flawless, and deeply personal. Certified in Wella Master Color, L'Oréal Professionnel Advanced, and Vidal Sassoon Cutting Geometry, she has shaped the aesthetic of Prague's most discerning clients.

K

Senior Balayage Specialist

Karolína Nováková

Karolína trained under Parisian balayage pioneers before returning to Prague with a refined hand-painting technique that produces seamlessly blended, luminous results. Her specialty lies in seamless blonde transformations and custom toning rituals that feel effortlessly natural and maintain integrity over time.

T

Hair Reconstruction Expert

Tereza Procházková

Tereza brings a scientific approach to hair health. A certified Olaplex specialist and keratin treatment expert, she diagnoses hair condition at a molecular level and prescribes custom reconstruction protocols. Her clients include professional athletes, television personalities, and those recovering from medical hair loss treatments.
